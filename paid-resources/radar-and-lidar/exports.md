# Exports



## Client

***

### _addBolo_

***

Flag the plate of a vehicle

```lua
exports.brazzers_radar:addBolo(plate)
```

* plate: `string`
  * BRAZZERS

### _removeBolo_

***

Remove the flagged status from a plate

```lua
exports.brazzers_radar:removeBolo(plate)
```

* plate: `string`
  * BRAZZERS

### _getBolos_

***

```lua
local bolos = exports.brazzers_radar:getBolos()
```
